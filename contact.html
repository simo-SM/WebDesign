Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about working together

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary depending on scope and complexity. A typical website design takes 4-6 weeks, while branding projects usually take 2-3 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

Do you work with international clients?

Absolutely! I work with clients worldwide and am experienced in managing projects across different time zones. Communication is key, and I adapt my schedule to ensure smooth collaboration.

What's included in your design process?

My process includes discovery and research, wireframing, visual design, prototyping, and final delivery. You'll receive regular updates, have opportunities for feedback, and get all necessary files and documentation.

How do you handle revisions?

I include up to 3 rounds of revisions in all my projects. This ensures we can refine the design to perfectly match your vision while maintaining project efficiency.

What are your payment terms?

I typically work with a 50% deposit to start the project and 50% upon completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone-based payments. I accept bank transfers and major payment platforms.
